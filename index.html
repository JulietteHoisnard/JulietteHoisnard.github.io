ABOUT

Hi, I'm Juliette! I'm French and I'm currently living in Berlin. After 2.5 years as Project Quality Engineer in France, it was time for me to move abroad and recenter my career to something I love! I embraced Le Wagon Web Development training in Berlin this summer. I am currently working as a full-stack developer in freelancing. I am mainly involved in 2tons, a French project currently turning into a real business. It's about a serious game to reduce your carbon footprint by taking collective and individual actions. I work for them in front-end in React / JavaScript and a bit of Redux. At the same time, I am also a part-time teacher assistant at Le Wagon.

I love Berlin, especially for cycling around. A fun fact is that I started road cycling because every sport clubs were closed due to Corona crisis. Otherwise, when dance studios are open and safe, I'm a dance addict, from salsa to ballet.

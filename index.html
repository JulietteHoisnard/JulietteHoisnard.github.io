ABOUT

Hi, I'm Juliette! I'm French and I have been living in Berlin for almost 2 years now. Big changes happened to me in 2020: I changed country and career, among many other things. After 2.5 years as Project Quality Engineer in France, it was time for me to move abroad and recenter my career to something I love!

I did Le Wagon Web Development training in Berlin in summer 2020. I then worked as a Frontend Developer in React.js / Redux during 6 months for 2tonnes, a French company building a serious game on carbon footprint reduction. I am now working as a Frontend Developer in JavaScript at Inno-Focus Businessconsulting GmbH, working on on the core product Inno-Ecos, a platform connecting multiple stakeholders of a business and adapted to each customer needs with multiple small apps.

I love Berlin, especially for cycling around. A fun fact is that I started road cycling because every sport clubs were closed due to Corona crisis. I did more than 3000km in 2020, similar in 2021. I participated to cycling events such as the NWC challenge in Berlin in 2020 (100km) and in Paris in 2021 (208km). Otherwise, I'm a really active person and I'm a dance addict, from salsa to ballet, as well as a yogi.
